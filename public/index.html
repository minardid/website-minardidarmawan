About Me

Experienced Product Manager with over 7 years of proven success in launching and scaling digital products across a range of industries, including healthcare, human resources, and technology solutions. Skilled in leading cross-functional teams to deliver exceptional product experiences, conducting market research to drive strategic decision-making, and implementing Agile methodologies to accelerate product innovation and revenue growth.

In healthcare, I have experience developing and launching digital health solutions that provides better experience and accessibility to health care through engaged member advocacy, connected administration, and curated employee benefits. In human resources, I have worked on products that enhance employee engagement and streamline HR processes, such as mobile attendance system and payroll management. In technology solutions, I have launched products that help businesses to automate their operations, optimize their workflows, and increase their efficiency.

Throughout my career, I have demonstrated a data-driven approach to product management, leveraging customer feedback, user testing, and market trends to drive product performance and ensure customer satisfaction. I thrive in fast-paced, dynamic environments and am passionate about creating innovative solutions that meet customer needs and exceed business goals.
